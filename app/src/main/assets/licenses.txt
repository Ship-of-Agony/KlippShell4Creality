Ideas, Sources, and Third-Party Open Source Information:

1. Core Inspiration
This application is heavily inspired by the great project "KlippHub" by DnG Crafts. This sparked my vision of being able to conveniently monitor my 3D printer on the TV right from the comfort of my couch. Special thanks go to DnG for the tireless support, as well as for all the other valuable contributions—such as the "Cfs RFID" application—for the entire 3D printing community!
