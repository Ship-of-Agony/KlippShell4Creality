7. Core Inspiration & Acknowledgements
This application is heavily inspired by the great project "KlippHub" by DnG Crafts. This sparked the vision of being able to conveniently monitor a 3D printer on the TV right from the comfort of the couch.
Special thanks go to DnG for the tireless support, as well as for all the other valuable contributions (such as the "Cfs RFID" application) for the entire 3D printing community!
